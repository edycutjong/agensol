The Problem

AI Agents lack verifiable on-chain identity.

  • Fragmentation: Wallets and reputations are siloed.
  • Trustless Revocation: No standard way to "kill" a rogue agent's access.
  • UX Friction: Humans struggle to differentiate bots from humans.

The Solution

Leveraging Solana Name Service for AI.
